About me

I'm a soon-to-be University of Warsaw graduate majoring in Architecture of Information Spaces. I’m passionate about User Experience, Design, and Usability. My Bachelor thesis revolves around usability in pixel art games, full title: “Usability heuristics for computer games with pixel art graphics”. Thanks to my studies and my current workplace I had a few opportunities to work on UX-oriented projects and expand my knowledge. I also have some experience in data analysis and audio engineering. This website is a form of portfolio, a place which allows me to showcase some of the projects I worked on and some topics that interest me. Apart from work related stuff I like to devote myself to analog photography, music producing and recently cooking. Thank you for stopping by and please feel free to contact me about anything. Have a good day!
