case study

subiektywnie
o finansach

This study examines the enhancement of user experience on subiektywnieofinansach.pl, a financial news service. Focused on refining navigation and content organization, the project utilized Information Architecture, competitor analysis, and UX best practices. Through a systematic deduction-induction approach, categories were streamlined from 77 to 21 and tags from 1503 to 45, resulting in improved engagement, reduced bounce rates, and intuitive content discovery
