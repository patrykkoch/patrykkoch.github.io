Graphic Design

Dziennik Gazeta Prawna (DGP)

Hello, I'm a graphic designer working for a polish newspaper Dziennik Gazeta Prawna. I create graphics for various purposes such as cover illustrations for the newspaper, illustrations for articles, animations, infographics, graphics for social media or events. My job requires me to meet constant deadlines, which means I have to be determined and quick when doing my graphic work. Despite this, with each piece I try my best to convey the subject matter and help visually direct the viewer's attention. If you have any inquiries, please feel free to contact me :)
